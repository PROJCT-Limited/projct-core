Current project

Infinite Maze 189
with Wit Pimkanchanapong

Wit Pimkanchanapong’s 4th edition of the Infinite Maze emerges as an expanding network of infinite combinations. Located in Sai Ying Pun 189 Queen’s street, the installation shows panels shift, lines of light reconfigure, and the grid itself breathes. Each movement alters the possible routes through the space, creating a living maze that is constantly rewriting its own map.

For PROJCT, this work activates the space with intention: a collaboration that brings together architecture, technology, and a precise yet playful sense of structure. The result is not just an installation, but an evolving system, one that asks how many access points we can find, and how often we are willing to begin again.

Location: G/F, 189 Queen's Rd W, Sai Ying Pun, Hong Kong

Date: 15 May– 17 July
