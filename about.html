Our Objective

The PROJCT platform is focused on helping founders, investors and corporates build and scale their ventures in a sustainable way. We partner in those journeys by going all in and bringing our network of diverse and global talent.

We believe the future doesn’t just happen to us, we actively shape it with research, experimentation, and purpose. Unleashing the creativity and potential of partners is at our core to enable people, projects, and society to grow.

Our Team
The PROJCT core team is mainly based in Hong Kong, with individual members based in Shanghai, London, and San Francisco. We have built a portfolio of complementary skills across strategy, creativity, research and tech. Passion is inherent to our work, and a commitment to quality drives the end result.

Space

PROJCT spaces are evolving with the function of the team. From the beginnings in the 2nd floor of an apartment building to two ground floor event and creative spaces, to our current location in a former super market on Queens Road West in Hong Kong.

Each space fosters strategic work, co-creation and an open invitation to all communities.

Code of ethics

At Projct, our commitment to integrity, quality and respect is the cornerstone of our work. As a company dedicated to empowering people and communities, we recognise that our success is built on the trust and confidence of our clients, partners, investors and employees. We hold ourselves accountable not just to financial profit, but also to our social and environmental impact.

“We enable people to live the life they want through co-creating better experiences, ventures, policies, networks, and financial models — while taking care of others and the planet.”
